Perro Caliente

Ingredients

  • Hot dog bun
  • Sausage
  • Mustard
  • ketchup
  • Option topping

Preparation Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add the hot dog sausage and cook for about 5 minutes.
  2. Preheat your grill. Grill the hot dog for about 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally until it's nicely browned.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add the hot dog and cook for about 5 minutes, turning occasionally until it's browned to your liking.
  4. If you prefer a toasted bun, lightly butter the inside of the hot dog bun and toast it in a skillet over medium heat until slightly golden.
  5. Place the cooked hot dog in the bun. Add mustard, ketchup, and any other toppings of your choice.
  6. Serve the hot dog immediately, perhaps with a side of chips, pickles, or a simple salad.
